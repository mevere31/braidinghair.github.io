About the Website Owner

I chose to create this website based off of my interest in black haircare. I used to work in a hair salon and often switch up my own hair with styles using synthetic hair. I found the recent news about this study disheartening but was really excited to use this project as an opportunity to do further research.

Thank you for visting my site! - Mikala Everett
